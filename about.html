Our mission

Osmos exists to democratise retail media technology. Every retailer — regardless of size — deserves access to the same ad monetisation tools that power Amazon's $50B+ advertising business.

We believe retail media should be a profit centre, not a cost centre. Our platform helps retailers go from 0.5% GMV to 1.5%+ within months.
